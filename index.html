TypeRight Keyboard — Privacy Policy

Developer: SY5TEM N3XT  ·  App: TypeRight Keyboard  ·  Package: com.sy5temnext.typeright  ·  Effective Date: June 15, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 20, 2026

The short version: TypeRight does not log, store, transmit, or share your keystrokes. Everything you type stays on your device. The only time any text leaves your device is when YOU manually trigger an AI feature — and it goes directly to your own OpenAI account, not to us. TypeRight is free and supported by banner ads served by Google AdMob.

1. WHO WE ARE

TypeRight Keyboard is developed and published by SY5TEM N3XT. This Privacy Policy describes how TypeRight handles your information. By installing and using TypeRight, you agree to this policy.

Contact us at: sy5temn3xt@proton.me

2. WHAT TYPERIGHT DOES NOT DO — KEYBOARD DATA

TypeRight does NOT:
  • Log, record, or store your keystrokes or typed text
  • Transmit your typed text to SY5TEM N3XT or any server we control
  • Monitor, track, or analyze what you type
  • Collect passwords, PINs, payment card numbers, or any sensitive input
  • Share any typing data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Send any keystroke data to SY5TEM N3XT at any time for any reason

TypeRight is an input method editor (IME) that runs entirely on your device. Your keystrokes are processed locally to render keys, apply autocorrect, and provide spelling suggestions. None of this processing leaves your phone.

AI is disabled automatically on password and secure input fields. When TypeRight detects a password field, PIN field, or any field marked secure, it disables all AI features for that session. No text from secure fields is ever processed by AI or transmitted anywhere.

3. DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE (LOCAL ONLY)

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to SY5TEM N3XT:

  • Your OpenAI API Key — If you choose to enter one, it is saved locally in your app's private storage. It is never sent to SY5TEM N3XT. It is only sent directly to OpenAI's servers when you trigger an AI feature.
  • Clipboard History — TypeRight maintains a local clipboard history (last 5 items) to let you quickly re-paste recent content. This data never leaves your device.
  • Personal Dictionary — Words you add or that TypeRight learns through your typing patterns are stored locally to improve autocorrect. Never transmitted.
  • NPUT Memory — If you use the NPUT AI assistant, it may store your name and personal facts you explicitly ask it to remember (e.g. "remember that..."). This data is stored locally on your device only and is included in AI requests sent to OpenAI when you use NPUT chat. It is never sent to SY5TEM N3XT. You can delete it at any time from the NPUT settings panel.
  • NPUT Reminders — Reminders and alerts you set through NPUT are stored locally on your device. Reminder content is used to generate AI-powered alert messages at the time you set the reminder; those requests go directly to OpenAI. No reminder data is sent to SY5TEM N3XT.
  • Settings and Preferences — Your chosen skin, haptic strength, glow speed, emoji usage frequency, and other preferences are stored locally. Never transmitted.

4. AI FEATURES — THIRD-PARTY DATA SHARING (OPENAI)

The only situation in which text from your device is transmitted externally is when you manually trigger an AI feature. All AI communication goes directly to OpenAI — never through SY5TEM N3XT servers.

TypeRight offers optional AI-powered features powered by OpenAI. These features require you to create your own OpenAI account and provide your own API key.

AI Reply and AI Rewrite

Long-press the A key on the TypeRight keyboard to open the AI bar. Tapping REPLY or REWRITE sends the relevant text from your current field directly to OpenAI's API to generate a response. You initiate this manually every time — nothing is sent automatically.

NPUT Chat

NPUT is a full AI chat assistant built into TypeRight. When you send a message in NPUT, your conversation history and any saved NPUT memory (name, personal facts) are sent to OpenAI's API to generate a response. This only occurs when you actively send a message.

NPUT Reminders

When you ask NPUT to set a reminder, TypeRight sends the reminder topic to OpenAI to generate a personalized alert message (a fact, a tip, and a personal note related to the reminder). This is a one-time call at the time you set the reminder. The alarm itself fires locally on your device — no data is transmitted when the alarm goes off.

Important: Your API key and any text sent to OpenAI goes directly from your device to OpenAI's servers. SY5TEM N3XT does not act as a proxy, does not receive this data, and does not store it. Your use of OpenAI is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Any charges incurred with OpenAI through your use of these features are between you and OpenAI. SY5TEM N3XT receives no payment and has no visibility into your OpenAI usage or billing.

5. ADVERTISING — GOOGLE ADMOB

TypeRight is a free app supported by advertisements served by Google AdMob. Ads appear in two places:

  • Suggestion bar banner — A small banner ad appears in the keyboard suggestion bar only when you are not actively typing. It disappears the moment you start typing and is never shown on password or secure input fields.
  • NPUT chat native ads — A native ad card is inserted into the NPUT chat thread approximately every 5 completed exchanges. These are styled to match the chat interface.

AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Coarse location (if permitted by your device settings)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

Where required by law (such as in the EU/EEA under GDPR, or the UK), TypeRight displays a consent dialog before loading ads. Your consent choices are respected and stored by the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK.

TypeRight does not share your typed text, keystrokes, or personal information with AdMob. AdMob's data collection is limited to what the AdMob SDK collects independently. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and Google AdMob's data practices.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time in your Android device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

6. PERMISSIONS USED AND WHY

  • INTERNET — Required for optional AI features that communicate with OpenAI, and for loading banner ads via AdMob. If you do not use AI features and ads are not loaded, this permission is not exercised.
  • VIBRATE — Used for haptic key-press feedback. Controlled entirely by your settings. No data is collected.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Required to deliver NPUT reminder notifications. Only requested if you use the NPUT reminder feature. You may deny this permission; NPUT reminders will not function without it.
  • USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT — Required to display NPUT alerts as a full-screen takeover on your lock screen when a reminder fires. Only used when a NPUT alarm triggers.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — Required for NPUT reminders to fire at the exact time you specify. Without this, reminders may be delayed by the system.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Required to reschedule your NPUT reminders automatically after your device restarts. Without this, reminders set before a reboot would be lost.

TypeRight does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, files, or any other sensitive device resource beyond what is listed above.

7. DATA COLLECTED BY SY5TEM N3XT

SY5TEM N3XT collects no personal data from TypeRight users.

We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or any tracking tools that report data back to us. The only third-party SDKs in the app are OpenAI (user-triggered, BYOK) and Google AdMob (advertising). Both are described in detail above.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

TypeRight is a general-purpose keyboard application not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

The optional AI features in TypeRight involve communication with OpenAI, a third-party service. Users under the age of 18 must obtain parental or guardian permission before using TypeRight's AI features and before creating an OpenAI account or entering an API key. TypeRight's in-app disclaimer requires acknowledgment of this requirement before the API key field is unlocked.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has used AI features without permission, please contact us at sy5temn3xt@proton.me and we will assist you.

9. SECURITY

TypeRight stores all local data in your device's private application storage, which is protected by Android's built-in sandboxing. No other application can access TypeRight's stored data unless your device is rooted.

All communication with OpenAI's API uses HTTPS (TLS) encryption. SY5TEM N3XT never receives, handles, or stores your API key or any text you send to OpenAI.

10. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

All data stored by TypeRight (clipboard history, personal dictionary, NPUT memory, NPUT reminders, settings, API key) exists only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

You can clear your API key at any time: TypeRight Settings → AI tab → clear the key → Save.
You can clear NPUT memory at any time: open NPUT chat → tap ⚙ → CLEAR ALL MEMORY.

Because SY5TEM N3XT collects no data, there is no data held by us to request deletion of.
