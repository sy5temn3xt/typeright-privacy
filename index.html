TypeRight Keyboard — Privacy Policy

Developer: SY5TEM N3XT  ·  App: TypeRight Keyboard  ·  Package: com.sy5temnext.typeright  ·  Effective Date: June 15, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 15, 2026

The short version: TypeRight does not log, store, transmit, or share your keystrokes. Everything you type stays on your device. The only time any text leaves your device is when YOU manually trigger the AI Reply or Rewrite feature — and it goes directly to your own OpenAI account, not to us.

1. WHO WE ARE

TypeRight Keyboard is developed and published by SY5TEM N3XT. This Privacy Policy describes how TypeRight handles your information. By installing and using TypeRight, you agree to this policy.

Contact us at: zakikhani.reza@gmail.com

2. WHAT TYPERIGHT DOES NOT DO — KEYBOARD DATA

TypeRight does NOT:
  • Log, record, or store your keystrokes or typed text
  • Transmit your typed text to SY5TEM N3XT or any server we control
  • Monitor, track, or analyze what you type
  • Collect passwords, PINs, payment card numbers, or any sensitive input
  • Share any typing data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Send any data to SY5TEM N3XT at any time for any reason

TypeRight is an input method editor (IME) that runs entirely on your device. Your keystrokes are processed locally to render keys, apply autocorrect, and provide spelling suggestions. None of this processing leaves your phone.

AI is disabled automatically on password and secure input fields. When TypeRight detects a password field, PIN field, or any field marked secure, it disables all AI features for that session. No text from secure fields is ever processed by AI or transmitted anywhere.

3. DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE (LOCAL ONLY)

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your OpenAI API Key — If you choose to enter one, it is saved locally in your app's private storage. It is never sent to SY5TEM N3XT. It is only sent directly to OpenAI's servers when you trigger an AI feature.
  • Clipboard History — TypeRight maintains a local clipboard history (last 5 items) to let you quickly re-paste recent content. This data never leaves your device.
  • Personal Dictionary — Words you add or that TypeRight learns through your typing patterns are stored locally to improve autocorrect. Never transmitted.
  • Settings and Preferences — Your chosen skin, haptic strength, glow speed, and other preferences are stored locally. Never transmitted.

4. AI FEATURES — THIRD-PARTY DATA SHARING (OPENAI)

The only situation in which text from your device leaves your device is when you manually trigger the AI Reply or Rewrite feature.

TypeRight offers optional AI-powered features (Reply and Rewrite) powered by OpenAI (ChatGPT / GPT models). These features require you to:

  • Create your own OpenAI account at openai.com
  • Obtain your own API key from OpenAI
  • Enter that API key into TypeRight's settings
  • Manually long-press the spacebar to activate the feature

What is sent to OpenAI:

  • AI Reply: The text you have copied to your clipboard (that you intend to reply to) is sent to OpenAI's API to generate a reply. You initiate this by long-pressing the spacebar and tapping REPLY.
  • AI Rewrite: The text you have typed in the current field is sent to OpenAI's API to generate a rewritten version. You initiate this by long-pressing the spacebar and tapping REWRITE.
  • AI Ticker: Generic content requests are sent to OpenAI to generate jokes, facts, and trivia for the idle suggestion bar. No personal text, typed content, or user information is included in these requests.

Important: Your API key and any text sent to OpenAI goes directly from your device to OpenAI's servers. SY5TEM N3XT does not act as a proxy, does not receive this data, and does not store it. Your use of OpenAI is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Any charges incurred with OpenAI through your use of these features are between you and OpenAI. SY5TEM N3XT receives no payment and has no visibility into your OpenAI usage or billing.

5. PERMISSIONS USED AND WHY

  • INTERNET — Required only for optional AI features that communicate with OpenAI. If you do not use AI features, this permission is never exercised.
  • VIBRATE — Used for haptic key-press feedback. Controlled entirely by your settings. No data is collected.

TypeRight does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, files, or any other sensitive device resource.

SY5TEM N3XT collects no data from TypeRight users. Zero.

We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, ad networks, or any third-party tracking in TypeRight. There are no SDKs in the app that collect or transmit data to us or to any third party on our behalf.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

TypeRight is a general-purpose keyboard application not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

The optional AI features in TypeRight involve communication with OpenAI, a third-party service. Users under the age of 18 must obtain parental or guardian permission before using TypeRight's AI features and before creating an OpenAI account or entering an API key. TypeRight's in-app disclaimer requires acknowledgment of this requirement before the API key field is unlocked.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has used AI features without permission, please contact us at zakikhani.reza@gmail.com and we will assist you.

8. SECURITY

TypeRight stores all local data in your device's private application storage, which is protected by Android's built-in sandboxing. No other application can access TypeRight's stored data unless your device is rooted.

All communication with OpenAI's API uses HTTPS (TLS) encryption. SY5TEM N3XT never receives, handles, or stores your API key.

9. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

All data stored by TypeRight (clipboard history, personal dictionary, settings, API key) exists only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

You can clear your API key at any time by opening TypeRight Settings → AI tab, clearing the key field, and tapping Save.

Because SY5TEM N3XT collects no data, there is no data held by us to request deletion of.

10. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

TypeRight integrates with one optional third-party service:

  • OpenAI (Privacy Policy) — Used for AI Reply, Rewrite, and Ticker features when an API key is provided by the user. Only activated by explicit user action. Not used at all if no API key is set.

TypeRight does not integrate with Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook, or any other analytics, advertising, or tracking service.

11. YOUR RIGHTS

Since SY5TEM N3XT does not collect or store any personal data from TypeRight users, traditional data subject rights (access, correction, portability, erasure) apply only to your local device data — which you fully control and can delete by uninstalling the app.

For any data shared with OpenAI through the AI features, please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy and exercise your rights directly with OpenAI.
